\section{How to use this appendix}

Assumptions are introduced \emph{in the chapters that need them}, with context, failure modes, and ``What Would Change This View'' disconfirmers.
This appendix collates them in one place for audit and safety-case work.
Each entry is one line; the cited chapter is the canonical source.

Lean bridge assumptions (\texttt{MB1}--\texttt{MB8}) are stated formally in Appendix~\ref{appi-lean-proof-spine}; the empirical validation program is in Appendix~\ref{apph-research-program}.
The full maintainer record (tests, claim links, uncertainty IDs) lives in \texttt{metadata/assumptions-ledger.md} in the repository (not printed here).
