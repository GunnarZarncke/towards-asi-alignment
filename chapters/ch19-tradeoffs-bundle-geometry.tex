\subsection{The Paternalism Boundary}

A system can protect humans while destroying autonomy, potential, dignity, or future correction capacity.

\textbf{[Defined]} Paternalism failure pattern:
\[
\Delta B_{\text{care}}>0
\quad\text{but}\quad
\Delta B_{\text{autonomy}},\;\Delta B_{\text{agency}},\;\Delta C_{\text{corr}}<0.
\]

Examples:
\begin{itemize}
  \item preventing suffering by flattening aspiration;
  \item preserving safety by eliminating risk-bearing agency;
  \item improving preferences by making humans easier to satisfy;
  \item benevolent domination without legitimate correction;
  \item value freezing disguised as care.
\end{itemize}

Paternalistic safety is a value-bundle tradeoff failure, not a separate book part.
