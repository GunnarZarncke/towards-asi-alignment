**Chapter theses (brief):**
- **Ch24:** Correction is a *causal channel*—human judgment must change future system behavior, not merely elicit compliance.
- **Ch25:** *Correction-channel integrity* (CCI) is measurable; alignment requires an informative, timely, authoritative, manipulation-resistant pathway.
- **Ch26:** *Extrapolative correction* goes beyond obedience—preserve humanity’s capacity to revise values and redirect the system.
- **Ch27:** The deepest failure is *manipulation/false consent*—changing the judge, not just ignoring correction.
