\part{Correction Channels}

\epigraph{%
Only variety can destroy variety.%
}{--- W.\ Ross Ashby, \emph{An Introduction to Cybernetics} (1956)}

This part makes correction the central causal object. It distinguishes obedience from preserved correction, defines correction-channel integrity, and studies how manipulation, domestication, and false consent can make a system look responsive while removing the human capacity to redirect it.
