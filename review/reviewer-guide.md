# Reviewer Guide

## Purpose

This book develops a conceptual and formal framework for superintelligence alignment. Reviewers should assess technical accuracy, internal coherence, and legibility for the intended audience tiers.

## Review types

1. **Technical** — formal models, equations, citations, claim strength
2. **Legibility** — operational definitions, decision triggers, artifact conductivity (see `context/legible-alignment-messageing.md`)
3. **Chapter** — completeness against the chapter requirements in `INSTRUCTIONS.md` §6

## Claim strength

Mark each major claim as:
- **established** — supported by evidence or standard literature
- **plausible** — reasonable hypothesis, not yet proven
- **speculative** — exploratory extension
- **philosophical limit** — outside technical resolution

## What to flag

- Terms used without operational definition
- Equations introduced without prose explanation
- Claims missing from `metadata/claims-ledger.md`
- Body text that relies on internal project names as shortcuts
